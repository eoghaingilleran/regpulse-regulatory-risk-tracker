Institutions shall have in place policies and procedures to ensure effective management of money laundering and terrorist financing risks.

Institutions shall conduct customer due diligence measures on a risk-sensitive basis and ensure that customer information is kept up to date.

Institutions shall monitor transactions to identify unusual or suspicious activity and shall submit suspicious transaction reports without delay where required.

Institutions shall ensure appropriate governance and oversight of AML/CFT frameworks, including clear allocation of responsibilities and regular review of controls.

Institutions shall maintain adequate records to demonstrate compliance with AML/CFT obligations and to support supervisory review.
